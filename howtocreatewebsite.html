Instructions on how to create website

First you will have to create a github account. Plese go to github.com

Follow the process to create an account.

After the account is created and you are logged into your account.
Click on the login icon on the top-right of the webstie and a dropdown menu will show up.
Click on "Your Repositories"

A new page will show up
You have to click on the green "new" button located on the right.
You will be prompted to provide a name of the Repository.
-Type the decired name of the repository you which to create on the textbox.
Click on the "create repository" green button.
From this point on your page is created. :-)
